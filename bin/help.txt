Create, install, and boot macOS virtual machines

  main.exe SUBCOMMAND

=== subcommands and flags ===

  boot                       . Boot an installed VM bundle in a window
  . -bundle DIR              . the VM bundle to boot
  . [-cpu-count N]           . virtual CPUs (default: 4)
  . [-memory-gib N]          . RAM in GiB (default: 8)
  install                    . Create a VM bundle and install macOS into it
  . -bundle DIR              . directory to hold the VM bundle
  . [-cpu-count N]           . virtual CPUs (default: the image minimum)
  . [-disk-size-gib N]       . main disk size in GiB
  . [-ipsw PATH]             . local restore image (default: download the latest
                               supported)
  . [-memory-gib N]          . RAM in GiB (default: the image minimum)
  run                        . Boot an installed VM bundle headlessly (no
                               window), then stop it
  . -bundle DIR              . the VM bundle to boot
  . [-cpu-count N]           . virtual CPUs (default: 4)
  . [-force]                 . force power-off instead of requesting a clean
                               shutdown
  . [-memory-gib N]          . RAM in GiB (default: 8)
  . [-seconds N]             . stop after N seconds (default: run until the
                               guest shuts itself down)
  version                    . print version information
  . [-build-info]            . print build info for this build
  . [-version]               . print the version of this build
  help                       . explain a given subcommand (perhaps recursively)
  . [-expand-dots]           . expand subcommands in recursive help
  . [-flags]                 . show flags as well in recursive help
  . [-recursive]             . show subcommands of subcommands, etc.
